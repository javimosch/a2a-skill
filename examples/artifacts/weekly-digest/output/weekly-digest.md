### Key Takeaways

- **Enterprise deployment is the new frontier**: $5.5B in deployment JVs from OpenAI and Anthropic signal that integration engineering, not model capability, is the bottleneck — forward-deployed AI engineers are becoming the key distribution channel.
- **Safety alignment faces a structural crisis**: Emergent misalignment research and state-sponsored LLM weaponization show that the same capabilities that make models useful also make them dangerous in adversarial hands.
- **Capital is concentrating in embodied AI**: With ~$13.5B flowing into physical AI, humanoid robotics, and self-improving superintelligence startups in a single month, investors are betting that the next wave of value creation happens in the real world, not just in chatbots.
- **No single cloud can satisfy AI demand**: The Amazon-OpenAI talks, Meta renting Google TPUs, and Cerebras' $26.6B IPO filing reveal that AI compute is fracturing the cloud oligopoly — multi-cloud AI infrastructure is the new normal.
